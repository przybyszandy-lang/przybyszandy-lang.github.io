Community Guidelines

Last updated: March 2026

DonkeyApp is a platform created for humour. The goal of this community is to share jokes and entertainment. The platform is not intended to be used for abuse, harassment, or derogatory political messaging.

1. Humour First

Content on DonkeyApp should be intended as humour. The platform exists to make people laugh and enjoy jokes shared by others.

2. Respect the Community

While humour can be bold and sometimes controversial, content that is primarily intended to harass, threaten, or abuse others is not allowed.

3. Prohibited Content

The following types of content are not allowed on DonkeyApp:

• content involving child exploitation or abuse
• explicit encouragement of violence
• direct harassment of individuals
• hate speech or racist content
• jokes about genocides or human tragedies presented in a derogatory way

4. Context Matters

We understand that humour can sometimes approach sensitive topics. However, content that crosses into clear abuse or discrimination will be removed.

Even if a joke may sound humorous to some audiences, it may still be removed if it violates these guidelines.

5. Moderation

DonkeyApp reserves the right to remove content that violates these guidelines or harms the community experience.

Repeated violations may lead to account restrictions or removal.

6. Reporting Issues

If you encounter content that violates these guidelines, please report it through the app so it can be reviewed.

7. Contact Us

If you have questions about these guidelines, please use the Contact Us page within the app.
