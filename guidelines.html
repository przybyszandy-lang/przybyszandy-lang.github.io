Community Guidelines

Keep it funny. Keep it respectful. Keep it sane.

Humour First No Abuse No Hate Moderated

How to use Donkey App properly

Donkey App is built for humour. The goal is to share jokes and entertainment, not to create conflict or hostility.

Comedy can push boundaries, but this platform is not a place for abuse, harassment, or harmful content.

1. Humour first

Content should be intended as humour. The platform exists to make people laugh.

2. Respect the community

Bold humour is fine. Content meant to harass or attack others is not.

Comedy is allowed. Abuse is not.

3. Prohibited content

The following is not allowed:

• child exploitation or abuse
• encouragement of violence
• direct harassment
• hate speech or racism
• jokes about tragedies in a derogatory way

4. Context matters

Some humour approaches sensitive topics. However, content crossing into clear abuse or discrimination will be removed.

5. Moderation

Donkey App may remove content at its discretion. Repeated violations may lead to account removal.

6. Reporting

Use the report function to flag problematic content.

7. Contact

Questions? Use the Contact page.
