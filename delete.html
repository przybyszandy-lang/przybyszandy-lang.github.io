Delete Your DonkeyApp Account

You can delete your DonkeyApp account at any time directly from within the app:

  • Open Settings
  • Select "Delete Account"
  • Confirm deletion

If you cannot access the app, you can request deletion by emailing:

andy@donkeyapp.com

Please include the email address used for your account.

What gets deleted

  • Your email address
  • Your display name
  • Your account information

Some content you submitted (such as jokes) may remain anonymised.
