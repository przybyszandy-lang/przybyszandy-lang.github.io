Cookie Policy

How cookies and similar technologies are used on DonkeyApp.com.

This Cookie Policy explains how Donkey App uses cookies, local storage, and similar technologies when you visit the website.

1. What are cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites function properly and improve user experience.

2. How cookies are used

  • Website functionality
  • Performance and analytics
  • User experience improvements
  • Advertising

3. Advertising

Donkey App may use Google advertising services such as AdSense or AdMob. These services may use cookies to display relevant ads and measure performance.

4. Third-party services

Third-party providers such as Google may collect anonymised usage data, including pages visited and general device information.

5. Local storage

The website may store data locally on your device, such as preferences, favourites, and cookie consent settings.

6. Managing cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings, including blocking or deleting them. Some features may not work correctly if cookies are disabled.

7. Changes

This policy may be updated from time to time.

8. Contact
