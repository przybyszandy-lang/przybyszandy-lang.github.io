Outlaws & Bandits

We respect no rules. We choose our own targets.

About the Alliance

Outlaws & Bandits is an alliance for players who make their own luck. We don’t play by anyone else’s rules — we attack anyone, anywhere, any time, as long as there is a good reason to strike. Strength, courage and initiative are what matter here.

We are a WarriorAlliance class alliance. Every member benefits from WarriorAlliance-class bonuses and plays with a clear purpose: grow stronger, strike smart and never apologise for winning.

Membership Requirements

  • You must be within the Top 300 players in the universe to join.
  • Exception: if you are marked as an Outlaw and have attacked someone stronger than you, you are also eligible.
  • We value bold moves and intelligent aggression above everything else.

Ranks Within the Alliance

We use military-style ranks for structure and coordination:

  • General — senior strategic command.
  • Colonel — leads major operations and coordinates squads.
  • Major — supports Colonels in planning and execution.
  • Lieutenant — frontline coordination and scouting.
  • Sergeant — experienced fighters and unit leaders.

Ranks exist to organise attacks and defence, not to control the fate of the alliance. No rank has the power to delete, disband or remove the alliance.

The Alliance Founder (me) is the only person who can remove or fully control the alliance at structural level. All strategic changes, including deletion or reformation of the alliance, are under my sole authority.

Rules of Communication

We may be Outlaws & Bandits, but we are not chaotic. Our communication rules are simple and effective:

  • Target Spotting: we actively help each other find good targets. If you see a possible victim with weak defences or bad fleet timing, share the intel.
  • Resource Sharing: if you find resources that you cannot take yourself, or a target that would be wasted on you, pass it on to another member. No profitable planet should be missed if one of us can reach it.
  • Recycling Opportunities: if someone in the alliance makes a big hit and cannot recycle everything, others should step in to help recycle and share reports where appropriate.
  • Politeness: we are polite to everyone — allies, neutrals and even enemies. You do not need insults or negative language to destroy planets. Our strength speaks for itself.
  • No Internal Drama: disagreements are handled in private chats, calmly and directly. We don’t waste time on drama when we could be farming resources instead.

Our Philosophy

We attack when we have a reason. We hit hard, we hit smart, and we stand by our members. If you like to live on the edge, strike above your weight, and respect strength more than titles, Outlaws & Bandits is your home.
