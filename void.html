Alliance Overview

Across the stars there are miners, traders and wanderers. Then there are those who rule. VOID DOMINION is not a shelter and not a farm. It is an aggressive war pact built on dominance, discipline and coordinated destruction.

We are not interested in hiding or begging for mercy. We are here to project power, protect our own and reshape the universe through calculated violence and smart strategy.

We do not ask the universe for permission. We shape it.

What We Stand For

  • Aggression with purpose – calculated raids and strategic warfare, not random suicide attacks.
  • Loyalty and unity – we stand together when it matters, and we do not abandon allies.
  • Respect inside the alliance – no ego wars, no pointless drama, no internal politics.
  • Knowledge sharing – we help each other grow stronger in fleet use, fleet saving and strategy.
  • Dominion through strength – we are not passive farms and we will not accept being treated as such.
  • Real teamwork – protection, revenge hits and coordinated firepower when our members are attacked.

We are not here to survive. We are here to control the void.

Alliance Rules

1) Be active

Ghost accounts and silent members do not last. You do not need to be online 24/7, but if you disappear for long periods with no word, you will be removed.

2) Respect your team

Inside VOID DOMINION we operate as one unit. Insults, constant drama and internal conflict are not tolerated.

3) Hit hard, hit smart

We play to win, not to throw fleets away. Scout, plan and coordinate. Use ACS, share intel and talk before large moves.

4) No begging

Ask for advice, not constant handouts. We help each other grow, but we do not carry players who refuse to help themselves.

5) Zero farm mentality

We build fleets and we hunt fleets. If you want to sit as a silent farm, you will not fit into VOID DOMINION.

6) War means war

When we are at war, everyone participates: attacks, support, recycling, intel. This is not a spectator alliance.

Playstyle

VOID DOMINION is an aggressive PvP / war-focused alliance. Raiding, crashing, ACS strikes and tactical planning are our standard operations.

If your idea of OGame is quietly hiding fleets and hoping not to be noticed, this is not the place for you. If you enjoy debris fields, calculated hits and reminding the universe who runs the galaxy, then you will feel at home.

Requirements To Join

  • Active player willing to log in regularly.
  • Team-player mindset – you act with the alliance, not just for yourself.
  • Interest in raiding, growing and coordinating attacks.
  • Ability to fleet-save, or willingness to learn quickly and listen to advice.

Points and rank can be built. Attitude and commitment decide whether you belong in the Dominion.

How To Join VOID DOMINION

If you want to fly under our banner and take part in coordinated dominance of the galaxy, contact our leadership in-game and send a short message introducing yourself.

Please include the following information in your application message:

  • Who you are as a player – tell us a little about your playstyle. Are you a fleeter, miner-raider, hybrid, or still learning the ropes?
  • Your current progress – share your current rank, points and rough activity level so we know where you are right now.
  • Your OGame goals – explain what you want to achieve. Are you here for coordinated ACS attacks, long-term growth, or total domination of your local area?
  • Your experience – if you have past alliance or universe experience, tell us about it. If not, that is fine; motivation matters more than history.
  • Why VOID DOMINION – what made you choose us and why do you want to be part of an aggressive pact built on dominion of space, not a silly sissy farm?

We are looking for active, disciplined players who want teamwork, strategy and coordinated power. Points can be built. Loyalty and mindset cannot be faked.
