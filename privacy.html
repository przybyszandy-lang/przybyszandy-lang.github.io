Privacy Policy

This website is a personal, non-profit project created for entertainment purposes. We collect only the minimum amount of information necessary to operate the joke submission feature.

Jokes

Any joke you submit will be stored permanently. This is required for the website to function and for jokes to be displayed to other users.

Email Addresses

Providing an email address is optional. You do not need to give your email address, and it is recommended that you do not.

If you choose to provide it, your email will be stored for up to one year and may be used only if you ever wish to be involved in future development of the project or to be recognised for your contribution.

Your email address will never be sold, shared, transferred, or used for marketing.

Data Use

All stored data remains private within this project. No profiling, tracking, analytics, or third-party advertising is used.

Your Rights

Updates
